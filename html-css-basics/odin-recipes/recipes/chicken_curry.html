Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a classic Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a flavorful onion-tomato gravy with aromatic spices. It is commonly served with rice or roti.

Ingridients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Cooking oil
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chilli powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until oil separates.
  4. Add chicken pieces and mix well.
  5. Cover and cook until chicken is tender.
  6. Garnish and serve hot.
