Vegetable Pulao

Description

Vegetable pulao is a fragrant Indian rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables and whole spices. It is easy to prepare and makes a wholesome one-pot meal.

Ingridients

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Onion
  • Green chilli
  • Whole spices
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and saut√© whole spices and onions.
  3. Add vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add rice, salt, and water.
  5. Cover and cook until rice is done.
  6. Fluff the rice and serve hot.
